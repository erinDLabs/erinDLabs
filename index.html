DIVVY Bike Analysis of Casual vs. Membership Riders.

Exploratory Analysis for December 2020 - November 2021 Historical Data

DETAILS COMING SOON

Divvy Bikes is a bike share company located in Chicago, IL which is managed by Lyft. Lyft offers open autonomous historical data. This exploratory analysis looks at the use difference between Casual riders who purchase either a single use pass, or a day pass which offers unlimited rides within 24 hours, and Membership riders which hold an annual pass with unlimited rides.
